Fried Eggs Recipe

The recipe to making fried eggs is very simple, but also very delicious.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heat up the pan and add in the butter
  2. When the butter is fully melted, and the pan is hot, crack open the eggs into the pan.
  3. Let the eggs cook until the outside is mostly white
  4. turn off the heat and flip the eggs over
  5. Only let the eggs cook for a few seconds (Around 10 seconds each egg)
  6. Serve the eggs onto a plate, and add salt to taste
  7. Done!
